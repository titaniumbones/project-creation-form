# Project Team

Assign team members to their roles on this project. Clear role definitions help ensure accountability and smooth collaboration.

## Role Definitions

### Project Owner (Required)
The primary decision-maker and accountable party for project success.
- Has final say on scope, priorities, and trade-offs
- Responsible for stakeholder communication
- Signs off on major deliverables

### Project Coordinator (Required)
Manages day-to-day project operations.
- Tracks progress and manages timeline
- Coordinates team activities
- Facilitates communication between team members
- First point of contact for project questions

### Technical Support
Handles technical implementation and data work.
- Develops and maintains technical solutions
- Performs data analysis and processing
- Ensures technical quality standards

### Communications Support
Manages external communications and stakeholder engagement.
- Drafts communications and reports
- Coordinates with external stakeholders
- Manages project documentation

### Oversight
Provides strategic guidance and quality assurance.
- Reviews deliverables for quality
- Provides subject matter expertise
- Escalation point for issues

### Other
Additional team members with specific responsibilities.
- Define their specific role in the notes

## Tips

- Every project needs both an Owner and Coordinator
- The same person can fill multiple roles on small projects
- Consider who needs to be informed vs. who is actively working
